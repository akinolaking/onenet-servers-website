FAQ

Common questions answered.

Can I migrate my existing website for free?
Yes. Every plan includes a free migration from your current host. Our team handles the full technical migration — files, databases, DNS, and email — with zero downtime. Simply raise a support ticket after signing up and we'll do the rest.
What is CloudLinux and why does it matter?
CloudLinux isolates each account into its own protected environment. If another site on the same server receives a traffic spike or is compromised, it cannot affect your CPU, memory, or disk — unlike traditional shared hosting where one bad neighbour ruins everyone's performance.
Is this cPanel or Plesk?
All web hosting plans use Plesk — not cPanel. Plesk is widely considered the more modern and secure control panel. It offers a cleaner interface, WordPress Toolkit for one-click WP management, and built-in security tools. If you're used to cPanel, the transition is very straightforward.
Can I pay in Nigerian naira?
Yes. Nigerian customers can pay in naira via Paystack — no international card required, no conversion fees from your end. UK and international customers pay in GBP or USD via Stripe. Pricing is displayed in your local currency automatically based on your location.
What happens if I exceed my bandwidth or storage?
We'll notify you before you hit any limits. You won't be automatically charged. You'll simply have the option to upgrade your plan or purchase additional resources. We never suspend a site for hitting resource limits without giving you fair notice first.
How does the 30-day money-back guarantee work?
If you're not happy within 30 days of your first purchase, simply contact our support team and we'll issue a full refund with no questions asked. No phone calls, no retention team, no obstacles. The guarantee applies to all new web hosting plan purchases.
